Premium Medical Specialties

Aura Integrative Medical Clinic coordinates multi-specialty expertise under one roof, analyzing the intersections of your biology. Our specialty blocks are governed by senior medical physicians certified with national qualifications and years of intensive tertiary care residency.

DEPARTMENT 01

Internal Medicine & Metabolic Management

Our core internal medicine department works continuously on non-invasive disease reversal frameworks, chronic illness control, and geriatric treatment pathways. Led by senior specialists, we utilize systemic biological mapping to discover why specific hormone systems fall into dysregulation. We do not stop at managing symptoms like glucose variance or arterial swelling; instead, we look into liver lipid processing, pancreatic reserves, cortisol secretion limits, and gut microbiotic integrity to resolve issues at their absolute core.

For patients with Type 2 Diabetes, our specialized structured lifestyle-reversal protocol tracks continuous glucose signals through real-time sensors, matching insulin spikes to unique nutritional profiles. In cases of chronic high blood pressure, we use detailed digital volume screening to identify arterial wall stiffness, crafting customized interventions that combine medication with physical recovery blueprints to gradually restore vessel elasticity.

  • Insulin Resistance & Diabetes Reversal Pathways
  • Hypertension Mapping & Visceral Fat Audits
  • Comprehensive Endocrine and Cortisol Protocols
  • Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome (PCOS) Recovery Loops
  • Geriatric Multi-System Care & Medication Minimization
  • Autoimmune System Moderation & Lifestyle Control
DEPARTMENT 02

Preventative Cardiology & Non-Invasive Diagnostics

Premature vascular aging remains one of the largest chronic health challenges across urban Maharashtra. At Aura, we approach heart disease through a lens of active detection and early non-invasive intervention. Rather than waiting for critical cardiac pathways to present symptoms, our preventative cardiology team works diligently to locate sub-clinical endothelial distress. We pair state-of-the-art diagnostic hardware with molecular lipid checks to target plaque deposition before it can cause structural vessel narrowing.

Our testing protocols feature computer-aided electrocardiogram (ECG) interpretations, stress treadmill profiling, and continuous 24-hour ambulatory Holter monitoring. Additionally, we analyze premium biological cardiovascular metrics, including high-sensitivity C-Reactive Protein (hs-CRP), Homocysteine tracking, and advanced apolipoprotein ratios (ApoA1 and ApoB). This comprehensive data enables us to craft protective lifestyle and supplemental maps, keeping your long-term arterial path clear and resilient.

  • High-Fidelity 12-Lead Computerized ECG Surveys
  • Sub-Clinical Plaque and Endothelial Distress Mapping
  • 24-Hour Ambulatory Holter Rhythm Monitoring
  • Homocysteine & High-Sensitivity CRP Profiling
  • Arterial Wall Elasticity & Micro-Vessels Analysis
  • Functional Cardiac Stress & Recovery Capacity Audits
DEPARTMENT 03

Advanced Pediatrics & Neonatal Wellness

Developmental pediatrics requires a careful balance of precise medical tracking and warm, nurturing care. Our pediatric department provides a quiet, sterile, and stress-free space designed to keep infants and children completely comfortable. Under the specialized care of senior consultants, we monitor growth curves, neuro-developmental markers, and child immunology milestones with absolute precision.

Our comprehensive pediatrics protocol features complete immunization tracking, developmental progress screening, childhood allergy mapping, and customized pediatric nutritional blueprints. By identifying digestive irregularities or early immune sensitivities, we help children establish a resilient metabolic foundation that supports a lifetime of healthy growth.

  • Accredited Immunization Management Schedules
  • Fine & Gross Motor Developmental Milestones Surveys
  • Early Pediatric Allergy Screening & Diet Mapping
  • Sensory Development & Speech Milestones Tracking
  • Pediatric Digestive Restoration & Nutritional Plans
  • Adolescent Behavioural Growth & Psychological Support
DEPARTMENT 04

Diagnostic Pathology & Advanced Chemistry Lab

Modern clinical decisions are only as good as the diagnostic data they are built upon. Our on-site computerized diagnostic pathology lab is built to meet strict clean-room standards, ensuring complete chemical and biological accuracy. By maintaining our own premium diagnostic facility, we completely bypass the risk of sample decay or handling errors that can occur during third-party transport.

Our laboratory features advanced computerized analyzers, calibrated daily using certified standard controls. Every sample undergoes multi-stage verification to construct high-fidelity biochemical profiles, covering detailed liver enzymes, metabolic markers, blood element distribution, and inflammatory indicators. Having these clean diagnostic results readily available allows our clinical team to refine active treatment plans with speed and absolute confidence.

  • Fully Integrated Local Symmetrical Testing bay
  • Daily Calibrated Computerized Chemistry Analysis
  • Hormonal Profiles & Precise Endocrine Fractions
  • Inflammatory Marker Verification & hs-CRP Screens
  • Liver, Kidney, & Complete Electrolyte Processing
  • Hematological Red & White Cell Sizing Metrics
