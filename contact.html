Patient Parking & Valley Services

Secure basement parking is fully available inside the ICC Trade Tower complex for all visiting patients. Authorized valet parking desk is situated at the main entry lobby of Floor 1 from Senapati Bapat Road for elderly patients and individuals with limited mobile capacity.

Wheelchair & Critical Access

The clinic spans Floor 1 and Floor 2 with complete, step-free elevator access. High-load patient transfer elevators are positioned next to the parking lobby towers. Direct critical-access stretchers are stationed at our emergency desk at the rear elevator lobby.
