Patient Q&A

Core Clinical Operations & Diagnostic FAQs

Our operational model is rooted in complete patient transparency. Below find explicit clarity on consulting pathways, sample testing cycles, billing configurations, and case management.

How do I book a premium diagnostic profile at Aura?
Appointments can be pre-scheduled via our dedicated Pune desk line or through the interactive intake portal on our contact page. We limit daily registrations to ensure zero waiting lines.
Does the clinic support direct cashless medical insurance panel tracking?
Yes, Aura Clinic coordinates directly with all major national health insurance providers and corporate wellness administrators for seamless, transparent processing.
What is the processing turnaround time for internal laboratory blood panels?
Thanks to our fully integrated, automated local diagnostic pathology wing, 90% of routine biochemical and metabolic profiles are verified and dispatched within 4 hours.
What makes integrative medicine different from traditional hospital care?
Integrative medicine doesn't just treat individual isolated symptoms. We map your complete biological history, environmental factors, and metabolic baselines to fix the underlying root causes of chronic illness.
