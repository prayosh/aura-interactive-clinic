Technical Protocols

Clean-Room Symmetrical Sizing & Lab Standards

Clinical accuracy is the foundation of effective medical treatment. To ensure absolute data precision, Aura Integrative Medical Clinic operates under a comprehensive, self-imposed commitment to high-standard diagnostics. We maintain a local, state-of-the-art pathology facility, keeping our testing processes entirely secure and controlled.

Our laboratory rooms maintain strict temperature and humidity standards to prevent any organic sample decay. We employ advanced double-barrier sample collection systems alongside full barcode scanning to eliminate any risk of sample mix-ups. Every morning, our automated chemistry and blood analyzers undergo strict multi-point calibrations using certified reference standard materials.

Our Three pillars of Symmetrical Diagnostics:

  • 1. Daily Machine Calibration Our computerized liquid arrays undergo rigorous calibration protocols using international chemical reference controls before processing any patient samples.
  • 2. Sterile Clean-Room Parameters All sampling chambers maintain a clean, positive-air pressure environment with advanced HEPA filtering to eliminate fine airborne particulates and cross-contamination hazards.
  • 3. Direct Sample Verification Every biological tube undergoes a strict automated barcoding process, managed by dual-consultant verification loops to ensure absolute sample integrity from collection to reporting.
